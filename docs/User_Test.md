# Wardrobe Manager - User Test

Hi, I’ve built a simple app to help people decide what to wear more easily.

This is an early version, and I’d really value your honest feedback.
This should take 5-10 minutes.

---

## 1. Access the App

https://wardrobe-manager-eight.vercel.app/

---

## 2. Scenario

Imagine it’s a weekday morning and you’re getting ready to go out (for example, work, lunch, or an event).

You want to quickly decide what to wear without spending too much time thinking about it.

Open the app and use it however you naturally would in that situation.

---

## 3. What to Do

There are no strict tasks to complete.

Explore the app in a way that feels natural to you. You can stop at any time.

If you’re unsure where to start, you might:

* Add some clothes you own
* Try putting together outfits
* Look through anything that seems relevant

The goal is to understand how you choose to use the app, not to complete specific steps.
